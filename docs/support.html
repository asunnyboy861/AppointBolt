Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Download AppointBolt from the App Store, create your business profile, and start adding appointments. The onboarding flow will guide you through setting up notification permissions and your first client.

How do I add my first appointment?

Tap the + button on the Today tab or Appointments tab. Fill in the client, service, date, time, and duration. You can also set reminder preferences for each appointment.

How do push notifications work?

AppointBolt sends local push notifications based on your configured reminder times. You can set multiple reminders per appointment (e.g., 1 day before, 2 hours before, 30 minutes before).

How do SMS reminders work?

SMS reminders are available on Pro and Pro Plus plans. They are sent through our secure backend and delivered directly to your client's phone number. Pro includes 50 SMS per month; Pro Plus includes unlimited SMS.

Can I customize reminder messages?

Yes! Pro and Pro Plus plans include custom message templates. Use variables like {client_name}, {service}, {business_name}, {date}, and {time} to personalize each message.

How does calendar sync work?

AppointBolt syncs with Apple Calendar using the EventKit framework. When you add or update an appointment, it automatically appears in your selected calendar. You can also import existing calendar events.

What is the no-show tracking feature?

When a client misses an appointment, you can mark them as a no-show. AppointBolt tracks no-show rates per client and provides analytics on how no-shows impact your revenue.

Subscription FAQ

How do I manage my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone or iPad
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Find AppointBolt and tap it
  5. Choose "Cancel Subscription" or change your plan

Or visit: https://apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions

How do I restore my purchases?

Open AppointBolt, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases". Your subscription status will be synced with your Apple ID.

What is the difference between Pro and Pro Plus?

Pro includes SMS reminders (50/month), email reminders, custom templates, and advanced analytics. Pro Plus adds unlimited SMS, team support for up to 3 staff members, an online booking page, and data export.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

You can continue using premium features until the end of your current billing period. After that, you will revert to the free plan with unlimited appointments, push notifications, and calendar sync.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! New subscribers get a 7-day free trial of Pro Monthly. You can cancel anytime during the trial without being charged.
