Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

Introduction

AppointBolt ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Business Profile: Business name, owner name, phone number, and email address that you enter during setup.
  • Client Data: Client names, phone numbers, email addresses, and appointment history that you manually enter.
  • Appointment Data: Service names, dates, times, durations, prices, and notes for your appointments.
  • Support Messages: Content you provide when contacting our support team.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and app version for compatibility and debugging.
  • Usage Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized data about how you use the app (no personally identifiable information).
  • Crash Reports: Automated crash reports to help us improve app stability.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect precise location data.
  • We do not access your photos or camera.
  • We do not track your browsing activity.
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the AppointBolt service, including sending appointment reminders.
  • To process in-app subscriptions and manage your account.
  • To sync your data across your devices via iCloud (if enabled).
  • To respond to your support requests and inquiries.
  • To improve our app through usage analytics and crash reporting.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Data Storage

Your data is primarily stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. If you enable iCloud sync, your data is encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account via CloudKit. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

SMS Reminders

When you send SMS reminders to your clients, phone numbers and message content are transmitted through our secure backend proxy to Twilio, our SMS service provider. Twilio processes this data solely to deliver the SMS messages. Message content is not stored on our servers after delivery.

Calendar Integration

AppointBolt uses Apple's EventKit framework to sync appointments with your device calendar. Calendar data is processed entirely on your device and is not sent to our servers. You can revoke calendar access at any time in your device Settings.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple iCloud/CloudKit: For optional data sync across devices.
  • Apple StoreKit: For processing in-app subscriptions.
  • Twilio: For delivering SMS reminder messages.

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing the use of your information.

Data Retention

Your data remains on your device and in your iCloud account for as long as you use the app. If you delete the app, all locally stored data is removed from your device. iCloud data can be managed through your iCloud settings.

Your Rights

  • You can access, modify, or delete your data at any time within the app.
  • You can disable iCloud sync in the app Settings.
  • You can revoke notification and calendar permissions in your device Settings.
  • You can request deletion of any support-related data by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

AppointBolt is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
